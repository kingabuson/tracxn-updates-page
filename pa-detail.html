Bring Tracxn data directly into your Google Cloud BigQuery

We are elated to expand our developer ecosystem with the launch of our BigQuery (Google Cloud) integration. Joining our existing suite of APIs, SFTP Dumps, and Snowflake offerings, this integration is designed for teams who need to blend Tracxn's deep market intelligence with their own internal data pipelines for large-scale analytics.

Documentation
Share

This new offering enables a more automated and robust data workflow for users within the Google Cloud ecosystem — moving from manual exports to direct, SQL-ready market intelligence inside your warehouse.

What does this integration offer?

  • Direct Analytics Hub Access: No more manual exports. Access Tracxn data directly in your BigQuery environment via the Google Cloud Analytics Hub for seamless data delivery.
  • Automated Data Integrity: Sophisticated Merge (Upsert/Delete) logic keeps your tables synced and clean. Whether you choose a daily or monthly schedule, your records stay updated and duplicate-free automatically.
  • SQL-Ready Bulk Analytics: APIs are perfect for real-time triggers; BigQuery is built for high-volume analysis. Blend Tracxn data with your internal datasets using standard SQL to uncover deeper market trends.

How to get started?

Setting up your BigQuery data pipeline is a collaborative process — we configure it together so the schema fits your exact analytics needs.

  1. Click on API & Dev Tools from the left navigation menu.
  2. Locate the BigQuery (Google Cloud) section and click "Learn More."
  3. Click the "Contact Specialist" button.
  4. Define your use case — tell us how you intend to use the data.

Alternatively, you can reach out directly to our team at apidesk@tracxn.com to begin the configuration.

Need help?

We've got you covered 24/7. Reach out via live chat, email at support@tracxn.com, or WhatsApp — we're always here to help.
