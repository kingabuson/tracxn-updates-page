Bring Tracxn data directly into your Google Cloud BigQuery

May 11, 2026 4 min read By the Tracxn team

We are elated to expand our developer ecosystem with the launch of our BigQuery (Google Cloud) integration. Joining our existing suite of APIs, SFTP Dumps, and Snowflake offerings, this integration is designed for teams who need to blend Tracxn's deep market intelligence with their own internal data pipelines for large-scale analytics.

What does this integration offer?

This new offering enables a more automated and robust data workflow for users within the Google Cloud ecosystem.

  • Direct Analytics Hub Access: No more manual exports. Access Tracxn data directly in your BigQuery environment via the Google Cloud Analytics Hub for seamless data delivery.
  • Automated Data Integrity: We use sophisticated Merge (Upsert/Delete) logic to keep your tables synced and clean. Whether you choose a daily or monthly schedule, your records stay updated and duplicate-free — automatically.
  • SQL-Ready Bulk Analytics: While APIs are perfect for real-time triggers, BigQuery is built for high-volume analysis. Blend Tracxn data with your internal datasets using standard SQL to uncover deeper market trends.
Built for analysts & data engineers

If your team already lives in BigQuery, this integration turns Tracxn into a first-class citizen of your analytics stack. Schedule daily syncs, write joins against your CRM data, and build dashboards on top of fresh market intelligence — all without exporting a single CSV.

How to get started

Setting up your BigQuery data pipeline is a collaborative process — we work with you to make sure the data fits your exact use case.

  1. Click on API & Dev Tools from the left navigation menu.
  2. Locate the BigQuery (Google Cloud) section and click Learn More.
  3. Click the Contact Specialist button.
  4. Define your use case — tell us how you intend to use the data, what fields you need, and your preferred sync schedule.

Alternatively, you can reach out directly to our team at apidesk@tracxn.com to begin the configuration. Most setups go live within 5 business days.

Why this matters

Until now, getting Tracxn data into BigQuery required scheduled API pulls or manual SFTP ingestion — workable, but brittle. The native Analytics Hub connection removes the plumbing layer entirely: you authenticate once, choose a schedule, and the data lands. The Upsert logic means re-runs are idempotent, so you can trust the table state without writing custom dedup scripts.

This also unlocks blended analytics that weren't practical before. Join Tracxn's funding-round dataset against your CRM's account-level pipeline to see which targets are about to raise. Layer our sector taxonomy over your portfolio companies to spot category exposure. Run cohort analyses across acquisitions data without leaving BigQuery.
